# EasyPort

**A smart and elegant World of Warcraft addon for instant teleportation detection and management.**

## üåü Features

- **Smart Detection** - Automatically detects teleport destinations mentioned in chat (dungeons, cities, hearthstones, and more)
- **Beautiful Banner** - Shows an elegant notification banner with dynamic icons when teleport options are detected
- **Carousel Interface** - Multiple teleport options? Navigate through them with arrow buttons instead of cluttered lists
- **One-Click Teleport** - Click the banner to instantly use the teleport spell or item
- **Real-Time Cooldowns** - Shows remaining cooldown time with visual feedback (greyed out icons and countdown timers)
- **Group Announcements** - Click on cooldown items while in a group to announce cooldown status to party/raid/instance chat
- **Works Everywhere** - Detects teleports in Say, Party, Raid, Guild, and Whisper channels
- **Smart Filtering** - Only shows for spells you know, items you have, and toys you've collected
- **Saved Position** - Drag the banner anywhere and it remembers your preferred location
- **Flexible Keywords** - Matches destination names with aliases (e.g., "dal" finds Dalaran teleports)

## üöÄ How to Use

1. **Type or see teleport destinations in chat** - The banner appears automatically when keywords match!
2. **Multiple options?** Use the arrow buttons below the icon to browse through available teleports (shows X/Y counter)
3. **Click the banner** to teleport instantly
4. **On cooldown?** The banner shows remaining time and clicking announces it to your group (if in party/raid/instance)
5. **Drag the banner** to move it anywhere - position is saved automatically
6. **Click the X button** to dismiss manually

### Examples:
- Type "dal" ‚Üí Shows Dalaran Hearthstone, Mage Teleport to Dalaran, Jaina's Locket, and Kirin Tor rings

- Type "garrison" ‚Üí Shows Garrison Hearthstone and Garrison Shipyard teleport
- Type "home" or "hearth" ‚Üí Shows your Hearthstone
